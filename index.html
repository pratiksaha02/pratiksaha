About me


Hi! my name is Pratik. 😊 I'm an 18 year old Photoshop artist. I live in India, Tripura. I did my schooling from Holy Cross School, Agartala.

My interest jumps from one field to another, like for example- video editing, photo editing, cyber security, web designing, and few more. Currently I'm working on my photo editing skills. I play with photos, and try to represent my imagination.
